Setup

  • select village size (2-4 fishers)
  • select number of players (1 - 4 players)

Rules

  • Each day, each player will have a turn to fish. Turn order is determined randomly at the start of each day.
  • Each turn, you choose the number of fish you will take that day. You are able to see the total fish available, and how many fish other players have taken.
  • You must eat one fish a day to survive. This will be automatically deducted from the total fish you catch. Any remaining fish will be sold, and 1 coin automatically added to your personal wealth for each fish sold.
  • At the end of each day, any player who has not been able to catch a single fish will starve, and be removed from the game.
  • If the fish population reaches zero, the game ends.

Goal

Your goal is to make it to the end of the game, having earned as many coins as possible
  • How do you earn coins? By selling fish.
  • How do you make it to the end of the game? By eating fish.
